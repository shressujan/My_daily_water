Background Water, water everywhere but not a drop to drink. This has become a very popular proverb in past few years. According to the article “How much water is there on earth?” published on USGS science for a changing world, “About 71 percent of the Earth's surface is water-covered, and the oceans hold about 96.5 percent of all Earth's water.”. That seems like a lot of water, right? While this is true, but we also can’t ignore the fact that only a few percentages of the water that’s on the earth surface is actually usable. According to the article from Jeremiah Castelo, “What is the Percentage of Drinkable Water on Earth?”, “Of the waters occupying 70% of the earth’s surface, only 3% is considered fresh water. Furthermore, about 2.6% of this freshwater is inaccessible for humans.” So, this only leaves us approximately 0.4% of the earth’s water which is actually usable for drinking and household purposes, and that percentage has to be shared among the 7 billion people in earth. And to make it even worse, the amount usable water is declining while the population around the world is increasing at a rapid rate and this makes it very hard to meet the water supply needs of the ever-growing population. This is why many countries, over the past few years, have already begun to face water supply shortage problems.

Introduction The idea of this project is to create a web-based database application that keeps track of water used for day to day activities. Since this will be a web-based app, a user would essentially require an internet connection and a computer/mobile device to use it. This app will be designed to be used as an awareness program that educates its’ users about their water consumption and the impacts of their use on the diminishing water resources. Based on their day to day inputs of their water consumption, this application will analyze their water usage activities and present the report to the respective user in terms of graphs and pictures to give them a visual representation, and the idea behind this is to help the user get a better understanding of their water usage activities and create an impact on them. The way this application is intended to be used is that, it will have a homepage or login page, which will require the users to login. This will be the first screen of this app and, if they don’t already have an account, then they will be given an option to sign up and create an account in a centralized online MySQL database which will run on a local server on my mac for now. Once the user is logged in, then the app will pull up the user’s info page which will contain user’s information like first name, last name, address etc. And, this page will give the users options to get their daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly statistics/report of their water usage, and along with that the users will also be given an option to view the water usage report for any city or state. They will also be given an option to view their water usage report within a certain timeframe to help them understand if their daily water usage has gone up or down in that timeframe. And, of course they can also do the same with the water usage report for a state, or a city. Users also need a place where they can log in their daily water usage activities like shower, kitchen, restroom, cleaning, garden hose, miscellaneous etc. And, this will be provided to them in a separate page.
